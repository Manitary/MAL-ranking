Dataset selection: the number denotes the minimum number of comparisons required for an anime to be included. Having anime without a lot of comparisons heavily impacts the speed of convergence of the parameters to their real values.

Further filter: the number denotes the minimum number of users that have the show in their completed or dropped list. This filter is applied after the computation of the parameter.


Meaning of the parameter:
If we take two anime A and B, the probability that user rates A more than B is P(A>B) = p_A / (p_A + p_B), where p_A and p_B are the parameters of A and B, respectively.
